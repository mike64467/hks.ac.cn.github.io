關於我們About Us

香港高等教育研究所 Hong Kong Institute of Higher Education Research (HKIHER)

作為一所獨立的全球性創新學術機構，香港高等教育研究所（HKIHER）以「融合科學與全球治理」為核心使命，聚焦教育理論與實踐、跨學科整合及前沿創新研究，致力於探索未來教育新範式與跨學科學術生態，為高等教育與學術研究領域的可持續發展提供獨立學術洞見與解決方案。

HKIHER is an independent global academic institute dedicated to knowledge integration and innovation. We focus on education theory and practice, interdisciplinary integration and frontier research, aiming to pioneer future-ready research paradigms and provide actionable insights for sustainable academic development.

使命任務Our Mission

  • 構建融合科學研究體系Build an integrated science research framework
  • 推動理論與實踐動態融合Bridge theory and practice
  • 賦能全球治理與可持續發展Enable global governance and sustainability
  • 引領跨域創新實踐探索Lead cross-domain innovation
  • 培育跨學科全球領軍人才Cultivate interdisciplinary leaders

研究領域Research Areas

  • 人文與社會科學Humanities & Social Sciences
    哲學、歷史、教育、社會與治理等，探討人與社會的深層機制。
    Explores philosophy, history, education, society and governance.
  • 人工智慧的跨學科應用Applied Artificial Intelligence
    推動 AI 與醫療、教育、金融、工業等領域融合創新。
    Integrates AI with healthcare, education, finance and industry.
  • 可持續健康科學Sustainable Health Science
    關注公共衛生、健康管理與環境影響的協同發展。
    Focuses on public health, health management and environmental impacts.
  • 跨學科前沿研究Interdisciplinary & Emerging Research
    融合多元方法，面向重大複雜議題的前瞻性探索。
    Forward-looking research addressing complex challenges.
